racheltomasetti.com Redesign Vision
Overview
A living document of mind evolution - a warm, minimalist editorial space where Rachel shares her journey of building Ki and personal transformation. Everything points to Ki, but this is the personal touchpoint, the garden where thoughts grow and connect.
Core Philosophy

Living document - Constantly evolving, not static
Mind evolution archive - Track the journey of thinking and building
Everything connects to Ki - All roads lead back to the work
Multimedia storytelling - Text, visuals, media mixed naturally
Explorable & discoverable - Easter eggs, hidden paths, intentional discovery

Design Aesthetic
Reference: Warm minimalist editorial (newspaper/magazine inspired)

Clean typography with generous white space
Warm, earthy color palette with accent colors:

Terra: #9e2a2b (brick red)
Ray: #efcb68 (golden yellow)
Pacific: #58a4b0 (teal blue)
Sage: #54783fff (olive green)
Dawn: #e0bad7 (dusty rose)


Serif headlines (Merriweather), sans-serif body (Poppins)
Content that breathes - not cramped or overwhelming
Mobile-responsive, elegant on all screens

Content Structure
Categories
Organize all content under these themes:

Build Log - Dev updates, Ki progress, technical decisions, building in public
Reflections - Personal evolution, distilled thoughts, philosophy, transformation journey
Stream Notes - Recaps and highlights from building streams
Guides - How-to's, frameworks, tools, resources for others
Ki Updates - Product announcements, feature drops, vision shares
Field Notes - Quick thoughts, fragments, observations, seeds
